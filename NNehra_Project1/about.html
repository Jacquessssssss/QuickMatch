How to Play QuickMatch

QuickMatch is a fun and engaging memory game designed to challenge your cognitive skills. Here's how to play:

Step 1: Click the "Play" button to start the game. Choose between Free Play or Timed Mode.
Step 2: Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs. Remember their positions to make matches faster.
Step 3: In Free Play, take your time to find all pairs. In Timed Mode, match as many pairs as you can before the timer runs out!
Step 4: After completing the game, check your score on the leaderboard and see how you rank against other players.
